Stay Safe – Our Tips & Guidelines

Emergency Contact Information

24/7 Front Desk: Dial 0

Emergency Services: Dial 911

Resort Security: Dial 1

Medical Assistance: Dial 2

Health & Wellness Essentials

Your room is equipped with:

  • First aid kit in the bathroom cabinet
  • List of nearby pharmacies and medical facilities
  • COVID-19 safety supplies
  • Emergency evacuation map

Safety Guidelines to Keep You Secure

  • Always carry your room key card
  • Use in-room safe for valuables
  • Keep your door locked at all times
  • Don't share room number with strangers

Resort Etiquette for a Smooth Stay

Help us maintain a pleasant environment:

  • Observe quiet hours (10 PM - 7 AM)
  • Follow pool and spa safety rules
  • Respect other guests' privacy
  • Smoke only in designated areas

Local Safety Tips You Should Know

Stay safe while exploring:

  • Use resort shuttle services when possible
  • Keep emergency numbers handy
  • Stay on marked trails during hikes
  • Be aware of local weather conditions
